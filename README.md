# TrustLedger
### Building Trust Beyond Credit Scores

> AI-Powered MSME Financial Health Card for IDBI Innovate Hackathon 2026 (Track 3)

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional MSME credit evaluation depends heavily on financial statements and credit history, leaving millions of New-to-Credit (NTC) and New-to-Bank (NTB) businesses underserved.

Although alternate financial signals such as GST, UPI transactions, Account Aggregator (AA), EPFO, and banking behaviour exist, there is no unified AI-driven framework to evaluate MSME financial health in real time.

---

## Our Solution

TrustLedger is an AI-powered Financial Health Card platform that aggregates multiple alternative financial data sources to generate a multidimensional **Trust Quotient (TQ™)**.

The platform helps banks:

- Evaluate credit-invisible MSMEs
- Improve loan approval decisions
- Reduce underwriting time
- Explain AI decisions transparently
- Increase financial inclusion

---

# Key Features

### AI Trust Quotient (TQ™)

A multidimensional financial health score generated using alternative financial data.

---

### MSME Financial Health Card

Visual dashboard showing:

- GST Compliance
- Cash Flow Health
- UPI Transaction Stability
- Liquidity
- Business Growth
- Repayment Capacity
- Risk Indicators

---

### Explainable AI

Instead of providing only a score, TrustLedger explains:

- Why the score was assigned
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Risk factors
- Recommended improvements

---

### Loan Recommendation Engine

Recommends suitable loan products based on:

- Business profile
- Financial health
- Cash flow
- Repayment capacity

---

### AI Financial Copilot

Conversational assistant helping MSMEs understand:

- Trust Score
- Loan eligibility
- Risk factors
- Financial improvement suggestions
